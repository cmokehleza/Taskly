FAQ

Got questions?
We've got answers.

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out — we're always happy to help.

Contact support
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
Yes — every paid plan includes a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. Full access to all features so you know exactly what you're getting before you commit.
Can I import from Jira or Trello?
Absolutely. One-click imports from Jira, Trello, Asana, Linear, and Monday — boards, tasks, assignees, and due dates migrate in under a minute.
How does team billing work?
Billed per seat, per month or year. When you add or remove members, your bill is automatically prorated. No hidden fees, ever.
Is my data secure?
Security is core to everything we build. 256-bit AES encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit. SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Enterprise plans add audit logs and custom data retention.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period. Your data is always exportable in one click.
Discounts for nonprofits or education?
We offer 50% off for verified nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact our sales team with proof of eligibility and we'll set you up right away.
